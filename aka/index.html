Why travelling is good:
Travelling and tourism is good for a multitude of reasons, for example:

  1. International travel allows you to experience a vast array of cultures, traditions, and histories.
  2. Traveling abroad can also have a positive impact on your mental health. It can help you reduce stress and anxiety and also can offer a sense of adventure.
  3. Seeing the world provides an education that's absolutely impossible to get in school.
  4. Travelling can help you become more independent and gain leadership qualities, especially if you're travelling alone.
  5. Tourism increases economic activity. The influx of tourists drives up local businesses' demand for services and products, creating jobs, increasing revenue, and reducing poverty.
